# Smart Launch Sequence

## Phase 1: Claim The Position

- Publish a clear landing page that explains the founder-after-exit thesis.
- Launch with 3 flagship essays, 1 partner note from PEF, and 1 issue roadmap page.
- Capture email signups for founders, sponsors, and contributors separately.

## Phase 2: Prove Taste

- Release a weekly editorial briefing for 6 weeks.
- Publish one iconic package: `The Body After Exit`, `The Family Office Without Becoming a Cliche`, or `What Founders Actually Do All Day After Liquidity`.
- Host two intimate dinners with PEF members and invited operators.

## Phase 3: Convert Community

- Introduce founding membership with editorial access, event priority, and private briefings.
- Launch a sponsor charter and take only 3 to 5 charter partners.
- Ship one printed pilot edition or collector-quality PDF issue.

## Phase 4: Build The Engine

- Add a city-guide franchise.
- Launch an annual summit.
- Start a private research product for founder-serving brands.

## Gating Rules

- Do not chase scale before the voice is unmistakable.
- Do not sell generic sponsorships just to create revenue optics.
- Do not build a community product that duplicates PEF without clear differentiation.
- Do not publish advice-heavy finance or medical content without source discipline and disclaimers.
