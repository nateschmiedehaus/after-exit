# Launch Roadmap

## Phase 1: Proof

- publish the visual landing pages
- define the first 12 story packages
- produce 3 flagship written features
- secure 1 short founder-film or embedded-life pilot
- open the founding partner deck

## Phase 2: Rooms

- launch intimate city salons
- recruit charter members
- publish photo-led event recaps
- test reader demand for member-only briefings and introductions

## Phase 3: Product Expansion

- launch paid member layer
- add short documentary films and embedded stories
- add sponsor-backed sector packs
- launch one retreat or destination experience

## Editorial Prototypes To Build First

1. `Life After the Wire Hits`
2. `The Modern Family Office for People Who Hate Cosplay`
3. `Founder Marriage Under New Conditions`
4. `Status Without Slop`
5. `Travel as a Life System`
6. `Embedded: Inside a Founder's Second Company`
