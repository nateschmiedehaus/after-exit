# PEF Partnership Model

## Recommended Setup

Build `Post Exit Review` as an independent editorial company with PEF as a strategic minority owner and founding partner.

## Why

PEF provides:

- exact audience fit
- launch distribution
- trust
- event and room seeding

The magazine must still retain enough independence to:

- attract outside contributors
- sign outside commercial partners
- preserve editorial credibility
- develop into a standalone asset

## Suggested Rules

1. editorial calendar belongs to the publication
2. partner content is clearly labeled
3. member-derived insights are anonymized by default
4. PEF co-hosts rooms and shares upside, but does not turn the publication into an internal bulletin
