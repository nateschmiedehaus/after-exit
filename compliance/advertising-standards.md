# Advertising Standards

## Principle

Advertising must make the publication more believable, not less.

## Accept

- clearly relevant founder-serving brands
- premium products with real category authority
- partnerships that benefit from context, events, or research

## Reject

- undisclosed affiliate schemes
- low-trust finance offers
- miracle wellness claims
- lead-gen pages disguised as editorial
- sponsors demanding story approval

## Disclosure

- sponsored work is labeled clearly
- partner research is labeled clearly
- editorial independence is stated publicly
- no member or reader data is sold
