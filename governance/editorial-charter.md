# Editorial Charter

## Purpose

`After Exit` exists to help founders build a more intelligent life after liquidity.

## Commitments

- Editorial decisions are made by the editorial team, not by sponsors.
- PEF can shape strategic direction as a founding partner without approving routine coverage.
- Reported pieces, essays, and partner programs are labeled honestly.
- The publication covers both aspiration and difficulty: performance, joy, status, boredom, family strain, and meaning.

## Decision Rights

- Editor in Chief: commissioning, story selection, standards
- Publisher: revenue, partnerships, events
- Founding Partner Council: annual strategy, budget guardrails, major hires

## Non-Negotiables

- no anonymous hype content
- no fake contrarianism
- no low-trust monetization
- no sponsor interference in editorial judgment
