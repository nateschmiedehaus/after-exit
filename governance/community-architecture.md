# Community Architecture

## Principle

The community should feel layered and private, not noisy and social-first.

## Three Layers

### Public

- flagship essays
- newsletter
- public event announcements
- selected films and photo essays

### Member-only

- issue deep dives
- city salon access
- private notes
- member directory
- curated introductions

### Invite-only

- founder circles
- retreats
- family office cohorts
- deal or philanthropic working groups
- host councils

## Community Mechanics

- small dinners over giant conferences
- introductions over open forums
- city captains over large local chapters
- recurring rituals over one-off networking
- confidential rooms over content spam

## Best Formats

- breakfast tables
- hosted dinners
- walking salons
- annual retreats
- private topic circles
- spouse and partner sessions
- founder-hosted home gatherings
