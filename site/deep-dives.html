Business Case

Why this can be a real company.

The case is not that magazines broadly are easy. The case is that premium niche media, memberships, and executive communities still work when the audience is wealthy, identity-rich, and hard to reach elsewhere. The clearest whitespace sits between Monocle, Long Angle, and TIGER 21, with PEF as the trust wedge and next-chapter principals as the broader category.

Media Reality

18%

Reuters Institute says only 18% pay for online news across 20 richer countries. Broad news is hard. Narrow value wins.

Niche Proof

5M+

Axios reported on March 11, 2025 that Substack had more than 5 million paid subscribers.

Community Proof

$34k

TIGER 21's North America membership dues as of April 2026. This audience already pays for trusted rooms.

Differentiation

Meaningful ways this differs from other magazines.

It needs sharper differentiation than "for entrepreneurs, but nicer." The moat is the combination, not any one element alone.
