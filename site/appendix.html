Evidence

Source buckets and analytic roles.

The dossier rests on grouped source sets rather than a single proof chain. Each bucket below identifies the sources used and the specific interpretive role each one plays in category definition, market sizing, price framing, room analogs, and physical distribution logic.

Audience and Wealth Base

These sources establish the affluent backdrop, the entrepreneur sub-layer, and the transition-stage operating problem.

This bucket is used to separate broad wealth from the narrower entrepreneurial-principal audience and to frame the move from company-building into allocation, family systems, and stewardship.

UBS Global Wealth Report 2025

PDF

Used to size the broad affluent environment and distinguish outer-ring wealth from the narrower target category.

Capgemini World Wealth Report 2025

Press release PDF

Used to anchor U.S. HNWI depth as the domestic premium spending base around memberships and partner categories.

UBS Global Entrepreneur Report 2025

PDF

Used to show that entrepreneurs reallocate time and attention toward travel, investing, family, and broader life design.

UBS Global Entrepreneur Report 2026

Overview

Used to support the view that many entrepreneurs are actively considering a next-stage transition within a defined time horizon.

UBS, From Entrepreneur to Investor

Article

Used to justify the broader audience architecture that includes founders, investors, and capital stewards in one frame.

Yale SOM Post-Exit Study

PDF

Used to define the post-liquidity decision set around identity, purpose, and reorientation after an exit.

NVCA / PitchBook Venture Monitor

Overview

Used to frame the recurring supply of newly liquid or transition-stage operators entering the market each year.

Knight Frank Wealth Report 2025

PDF

Used to map premium spending categories such as wellness, travel, education, and family experiences to partner fit.

Media Pricing and Product Form

These sources frame premium media economics, bundling behavior, and the limits of broad digital news payment.

This bucket is used to show where general news breaks down, where narrow premium media still holds pricing power, and how leading products ladder into higher-value access or tool layers.

Reuters Institute Digital News Report 2025

PDF

Used to establish that broad digital news payment remains structurally weak and that narrow identity-rich media is the more relevant reference set.

Axios Media Trends, March 11, 2025

Used to frame the current media environment around distinctiveness, personality, and mixed-format publishing.

The Information Pricing

Every

Used as the benchmark for bundling media with products, benefits, and community rather than selling content in isolation.

Air Mail

Subscription options

Used as a reference point for a premium editorial brand built around taste, service, and a coherent point of view.

Puck

Billing and Inner Circle

Used as a reference for subscription-plus-membership packaging with higher-touch access layers.

Semafor CEO Signal

Launch and Expansion

Used to show that executive-focused editorial products can extend into invitation-only and multimedia formats without losing clarity.

Print Artifact and Circulation Logic

These sources define cadence requirements, production choices, distribution channels, and advertiser reporting standards.

This bucket is used to treat the magazine as a real physical system rather than a visual concept: issue frequency, print specifications, controlled circulation, venue placement, and the distinction between paid and nonpaid distribution.

USPS Periodicals

Overview and frequency rule

Used to define the quarterly cadence requirement for a true periodical mailing model.

USPS Mailing Guidance

Additional entry and Periodicals manual

Used to define the operational mailing framework for subscriber delivery, regional entry, and compliance.

USPS Circulation Rulings

Requester publication ruling and circulation guidance

Used to keep paid circulation separate from controlled venue copies, event drops, and similar nonpaid channels.

Walsworth

Case study and paper stock guide

Used to map binding, page-count, and paper-finish decisions for a collectible rather than disposable object.

Sheridan Printing and Mailing

co-mail, publication logistics, and capabilities

Used to define the printer-fulfillment vendor profile required for quality, mailing support, and coordinated operations.

Kinfolk

Subscription

Used to benchmark subscriber-first print delivery for a premium editorial object.

Soho House Magazine

Launch, global placement, and sale channel

Used to benchmark a membership-linked print title distributed across rooms, venues, and commerce rather than retail newsstands alone.

Advertiser Context and Audit Standards

These sources define attention, context quality, and circulation credibility for partner-facing reporting.

This bucket is used to support the view that qualified physical environments can produce a stronger advertiser proposition than large but indistinct reach, provided the circulation categories are reported clearly.

MPA Magazine Media Factbook

Factbook

Used to anchor general print-magazine value arguments around trust, engagement, and print-media characteristics.

Newsworks Attention in a Quality Environment

Study

Used to support the relationship between quality editorial context and stronger advertiser attention outcomes.

News UK and Lumen Context Study

Study

Used to support the importance of contextual fit and relevance rather than raw impression scale.

AAM Overview

Overview and media audits

Used to frame the role of audited and segmented circulation reporting in partner credibility.

AAM Magazine Terms and Nonpaid Bulk Guidance

Terms and nonpaid bulk

Used to separate paid copies, public-place copies, and event or bulk distribution in reporting and partner packaging.

AAM Verified Public Place

Guidance

Used to define how hotel, lounge, waiting-room, and other venue placements can be classified and substantiated.

The source base is strongest when each bucket is used for its specific role: market size, audience transition, premium pricing, room analogs, print mechanics, or circulation credibility.
