# Brand Standards

## Editorial Mood

Elegant, exact, discreet, and highly visual.

The site and the magazine should feel closer to a private club annual review crossed with a premium issue launch than to a startup blog.

## What It Is Not

- not bro media
- not general business news
- not glossy luxury fluff
- not a PEF brochure

## Visual Rules

- lead with full-bleed photography
- prefer real environments to staged conference imagery
- use sparse copy
- keep graphics disciplined and editorial
- use video selectively and only when it proves the room or the life
