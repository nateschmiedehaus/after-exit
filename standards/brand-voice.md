# Brand Voice

## Tone

The voice should feel:

- calm
- expensive without bragging
- intimate without oversharing
- practical without sounding like LinkedIn sludge
- spiritually literate without becoming vague

## Editorial Rules

- Write for people who have already built something difficult.
- Never talk down to the reader.
- Prefer concrete choices over abstract inspiration.
- Avoid startup cliches, grindset language, and "crushing it" rhetoric.
- Use taste as a signal of seriousness, not snobbery.

## Visual Rules

- magazine, not SaaS
- poster-like first viewport
- serif-forward hierarchy
- warm paper palette with dark ink and one copper accent
- restrained motion
- no dashboard cards in marketing surfaces
