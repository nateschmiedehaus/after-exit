# Side Quests

These are the adjacent products that become plausible once the brand earns trust.

## Highest-Fit Extensions

1. `Embedded Lives`
   Photo essays and short films inside founders' startups, homes, training routines, and family-office builds.

2. `Founder Travel Club`
   Curated itineraries, annual trips, and destination-based member gatherings.

3. `Private Dining Network`
   City-by-city host network with chef, venue, and table guides.

4. `Founder Household Guide`
   Schooling, relocation, home operations, staffing, and security recommendations.

5. `Family Constitution Workshops`
   Programmed sessions around values, inheritance, rituals, and governance.

6. `Ghostwriting and Reputation Studio`
   Essays, speeches, founder notes, and tasteful self-presentation.

7. `Longevity Network`
   Vetted doctors, trainers, diagnostics, and recovery operators.

8. `Partner and Spouse Track`
   Relationship and family programming adjacent to the founder audience.

9. `Deal Salons`
   Small-format company presentations or operator-introduction circles.

10. `Annual City Guides`
    Wealth, health, hospitality, schools, clubs, and service networks in key founder cities.

## Why Side Quests Matter

The strongest media brands stop being just publications. They become filters for products and relationships that already want to happen.
