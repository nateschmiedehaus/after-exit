# Supply Chain

## Core Recommendation

Start lean and outsource specialists. Internalize only the pieces that define trust, pace, and taste.

## Launch Model

### Keep internal

- editor-in-chief or editorial lead
- partnerships lead
- community curator
- guest list and room curation

### Outsource early

- copy editing and fact checking
- photography pool
- video editing
- event production labor
- web production
- travel and concierge fulfillment

## Photo Strategy

The site and the publication should prefer documentary-style founder imagery over stock-looking business shots.

Best image categories:

- founder dinners and salons
- home libraries and studies
- terraces, airports, train stations, and travel movement
- training environments
- workshops and second-company build scenes
- family-table or hosting moments

## Video Strategy

Video should be selective.

Use it for:

- one manifesto trailer
- room proof from dinners and retreats
- short embedded founder documentaries
- marquee profile stories

Avoid turning every page into a video wall.

## Suggested Tools

- recording: [Riverside](https://riverside.fm/use-cases/podcasting)
- edit and cutdowns: [Descript](https://www.descript.com/)
- events and gated calendars: [Luma memberships](https://help.luma.com/p/calendar-memberships)
- community: [Circle memberships](https://circle.so/membership)
- payouts when needed: [Stripe Connect](https://stripe.com/connect/marketplaces)

## Avoid Early

- building a full newsroom
- staffing in-house concierge
- custom community software
- volume ad sales
- expensive original video series before demand exists
