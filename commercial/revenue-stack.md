# Revenue Stack

## Recommended Order

1. `Founding partnerships`
2. `Premium memberships`
3. `City salons`
4. `Partner intelligence products`
5. `Editorial studio`
6. `Retreats`
7. `Courses and playbooks`
8. `Referral marketplace`
9. `Annual summit`
10. `Software or licensed chapter systems`

## Near-Term Lines

### Founding partnerships

The best early revenue. Sell category rights and room access, not banner inventory.

Strong categories:

- wealth management
- private banking
- family governance
- longevity and wellness
- premium travel
- education
- residential and relocation services

### Premium memberships

The sale is not just content. The sale is access, filtering, and the right rooms.

Suggested tiers:

- `Reader`
- `Circle`
- `Patron`

### Intimate city salons

Small dinners and off-the-record roundtables are better early than big conferences. They create:

- sponsor inventory
- community density
- editorial material
- photos and video proof

### Partner intelligence products

Custom roundtables and anonymized founder insight memos for aligned brands can become fast, high-margin revenue.

## Later-Stage Lines

- founder travel club
- family office cohorts
- curated service marketplace
- spouse or partner programming
- annual print issue
- taste-led product collaborations
- reputation and ghostwriting services

## Why This Stack Works

It monetizes trust before scale. That is the right order for this audience.
