# Business Model

## Revenue Stack

1. `Membership`  
   Paid access to full archives, private briefings, founder salons, and issue drops.
2. `Strategic Sponsors`  
   Annual packages sold to a short list of founder-relevant brands.
3. `Events`  
   Dinners, summits, city salons, and destination weekends.
4. `Research`  
   Audience reports and executive insight packages for sponsor categories.
5. `Special Editions`  
   Print issues, city guides, and sponsored but clearly labeled editorial packages.

## Product Ladder

- free email briefing
- paid digital membership
- founding member tier
- summit and salon access
- bespoke research or partnership packages

## Commercial Principle

This business should optimize for `audience quality x trust x spend capacity`, not raw traffic.

That means:

- fewer advertisers
- higher sponsorship prices
- more direct relationships
- less programmatic clutter

## Charter Partner Targets

- private banking and wealth planning
- performance medicine and diagnostics
- travel and hospitality
- residential and design services
- high-trust productivity or advisory software for operators
